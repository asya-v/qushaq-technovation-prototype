Child is having a hard moment right now
Follow the steps. You can do this.
🫁 Take a deep breath first. Your calm matters more than any words right now. child can feel it.
Step by step
1
Pause and stay quiet
No questions, explanations, or commands. Silence right now is support, not indifference.
2
Leave the noisy place
Calmly and without rushing, guide your child to a quieter place with fewer people.
3
Get down to your child’s level
Crouch or sit nearby. Do not stand over them. That adds pressure. Just stay close.
4
Offer support and do not rush
Offer headphones, let them lean against a wall, or give them a soft object. Stay quiet. Your presence is already helping.
5
When breathing settles, say their name
Say quietly and calmly: “Child, I’m here.” Once. No rush.
